Work in Progress

Modern Football is currently in active development. What you see here is a glimpse of where we're headed.

The Vision

A football experience that pushes far past the usual boundaries of the genre. Built to feel authentic, tactical, and alive in ways traditional football games don't even attempt.

Modern Football is where we're testing what happens when you rebuild a sports game from scratch with no legacy baggage. Deep simulation, smarter AI, and gameplay that respects the sport instead of just copying what came before.

Built Different

Most football games are iterations. They're built on decades of assumptions about what a football game should be. We're asking: what if we started fresh?

What if the AI actually understood tactics? What if player behavior felt less scripted and more human? What if the game reacted to your decisions in ways that actually mattered?

Those are the questions we're trying to answer. No fluff, no gimmicks—just football that feels right.

Current Status

We're deep in development, experimenting with core systems and pushing the simulation further than we initially planned. It's taking time because we're not interested in shipping something half-baked.

When there's something worth showing, you'll see it here first.
